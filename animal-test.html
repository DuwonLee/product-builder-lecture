Animal Face Test

What does your face say about your animal double?

Our Animal Face Test uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) to recognize patterns and features in your photo, matching them with known animal characteristics. This model has been trained on thousands of images to provide a fun and surprisingly accurate reflection of your "animal type".

AI is analyzing your features...

How it works:

  • We use Teachable Machine, a Google platform for creating Machine Learning models.
  • Your photo is processed locally in your browser; it is never uploaded to our servers.
  • The model identifies features like eye shape, jawline, and proportions to calculate probabilities.
